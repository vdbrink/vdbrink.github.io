# Feed: Home Assistant - Mushroom Chip card

The power of a Mushroom chip card is that it's a small icon which you can define with custom icons, text, and colors based on the state of a sensor.

Many icons next to each other fit in a row on the dashboard, and you can also choose to only show them if they are relevant.

Example of chips I use:
* Person locations
* Number of people at home
* Current temperature outside
* Number of open windows
* What to wear outside
* Expected rain amount
* Rain intensity today
* Weather alarm
...
